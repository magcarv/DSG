Some of the results

Looking for a job

47,1% of Brazilian and 46,66% of LATAM respondents are looking for a new job.

Motivation

The biggest motivation for Brazilians to change jobs is to grow professionally, and for Latin Americans, it is getting a higher salary.

Senior professionals

Senior or upper level professionals are 54% in LATAM and 72% in Brazil.

Duration

68% of the Brazilian respondents with the highest salary rate have been working in the same company for up to 2 years

Fintech

Fintech is the industry that most Latin American digital professionals want to work in (29%).

New job

56% of the lowest and 25% of the highest salary range Brazilian respondents are looking for a new job.
